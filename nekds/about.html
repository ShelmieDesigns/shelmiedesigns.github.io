About our company

New England Kenyan Diaspora Sacco (NEKDS) is a duly registered and licensed non-Deposit Taking Sacco founded by Kenyans in the diaspora and established in 2016 with the sole objective of uplifting its membersâ€™ social-economic wellbeing. We are uniquely familiar to the challenges faced by Kenyans working overseas and are therefore driven by the relentless desire to address these challenges and ensure no other non-resident Kenyans lose their hard-earned income and also to create sustainable alternatives. Our products and services are tailored to the needs of these hardworking individuals .We also make investments with sustainable risk-adjusted returns. Our operating objective is thus to create a platform that affords our members convenient access to credit and facilitates wealth growth through interest earning savings and investments.

The socio-economic benefits

NEKDS is indeed its people and the values by which they live. We believe in collaborative approach to personal economic growth and development. Our products and services afford our members financial and investment opportunities previously inaccessible to non-residents Kenyans.

Our Mission

To be a strong member-centric financial Co-operative providing innovative financial services and products, while increasing the community savings among non-resident Kenyans. This we achieve by providing competitive loans and offering financial literacy programs to help our members make prudent sustainable investment and financial decisions.

Our Vision

To be a Sacco that is competitive, viable and sustainable in building capacity of our members to fulfill their socio-economic aspirations and obligations.

Our Objectives

At NEKDS we are committed to being a sound, progressive and a strong financial institution providing our members with superior financial services and products, while maintaining our traditional values anchored in sustainable use of Our Diaspora resources.

  • Must be a non-resident Kenyan aged above 18 years and gainfully employed
