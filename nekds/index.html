What we offer NEXT

CREDIT PRODUCTS

In line with our founding objective of improving the socio-economic well-being of our members, we provide loans and advances of varied tenors as may be determined by our Board of Directors from time to time. The amount granted to any one member shall range from Kshs 1000 to the maximum legal limit of 10% of our core capital and shall as well vary from time to time as may be determined and approved by our board.

Personal Development Loans

Repayment period shall vary on case by case basis as determined by the board;

A member must have been active and consistently saved with NEKDS for a period of not less than six (6) months;

A member shall qualify for a loan with their savings without security as long as he or she has not used the same savings to guarantee any other loan;

A member shall borrow up to three times his or her savings;

The interest rate shall be 1% per month on the reducing balance or as shall be determined by the Board of Directors from time to time;

Every loan shall attract a charge of 0.1% and shall be processed within a maximum of six (6) working days;

Members will be required to not only provide security to qualify for loans but also demonstrate ability to make repayments.

The total of all outstanding loans, including personal development loans, emergency and air ticket advance loans must not exceed three times a member’s total savings.

Savings Products

In pursuit of our vision to be a Sacco that is not only competitive but also viable and sustainable in building capacity of our members to fulfill their socio-economic goals and obligations, we offer a wide array of saving products and services.

Equity Capital/Shares

Each NEKDS member is required to upon registration purchase at least one thousand (1000)shares at Kshs 100 per share or at such price as may vary from time to time. Shares shall be payable within 2 years of membership and shall not be refundable or assignable as collateral for a loan, but a member may transfer his or her shares to other members on leaving membership of the Sacco.

Shares may earn dividends paid from net surplus after required transfers to reserves at the end of every financial year in accordance with the dividend policy of the NEKDS. A member in good standing should thus have at least Kshs 100,000 worth of shares.

OUR MEMBERS

NEKDS is a convergence of diverse individuals with one common interest-that of sustainably improving their socio-economic wellbeing. Our membership is thus open to non-resident Kenyans living and working around the globe keen on personal financial growth and development through convenient and hustle-free access to local investment opportunities in their motherland Kenya.

The preconditions for our membership

Must be a non-resident Kenyan aged above 18 years and gainfully employed;


Applicants are required to provide the following:

A copy of national identity card (ID) or Passport;

Recent colored Passport size photos;

Details on the designated nominee or next of kin.


Payment of a one-time non-refundable membership registration fee of Kshs 5,000 or USD equivalent by form of cash deposit or cheque;

Purchase of a minimum of 1000 shares at Kshs 100 per share within 2 years of joining NEKDS.

Our Story

New England Kenyan Diaspora Sacco (NEKDS) is a duly registered and licensed non-Deposit Taking Sacco founded by Kenyans in the diaspora and established in 2016 with the sole objective of uplifting its members’ social-economic wellbeing.

We are thus a Sacco of the diaspora, for the diaspora by the diaspora. We are uniquely close to the challenges faced by Kenyans working overseas. From fraud, lack of access to capital and credit, intricate market dynamics to weak management, our founders and management are intimately familiar with these challenges having experienced them themselves. We are inspired and ferociously driven by the relentless desire to not only address these challenges and ensure no other non-resident Kenyans undeservedly lose their hard-earned income but to also create sustainable alternatives. We therefore create products and services uniquely tailored to the needs of these hardworking individuals as well as make investments with sustainable risk-adjusted returns. Our operating objective is thus to not only create a platform that affords our members convenient access to credit but also facilitates wealth growth through interest earning savings and investments.

Our Inspiration

At NEKDS we believe in sustainable wealth growth and development. We are inspired by a burning desire to uplift the social and economic wellbeing of our members. Core to the realization of this endeavour is provision of easy access to credit and investment opportunities to our members thereby enabling them and freeing to pursue interests and opportunities that matter to them most. We leverage our every waking and working moment to make our Sacco a members-FIRST, members-DRIVEN, members-CENTRIC, members-FOCUSED and members-TRUSTED Sacco. Being a member-centric Sacco is thus absolutely imperative to us. It is about the profound opportunity we have to make our members our brand loyalists for life. The common thread in our entire product and service delivery is exceptional value and convenience. Our members need answers quickly and we are conscious of the fact that demand is perishable.

Our Mission: Our Mission is to be a strong member-centric financial Co-operative providing innovative financial services and products, while increasing the community savings among non-resident Kenyans. This we achieve by providing competitive loans and offering financial literacy programs to help our members make prudent sustainable investment and financial decisions.

Our Vision: Our vision is to be a Sacco that is competitive, viable and sustainable in building capacity of our members to fulfill their socio-economic aspirations and obligations.

Our Objectives: At NEKDS we are committed to being a sound, progressive and a strong financial institution providing our members with superior financial services and products, while maintaining our traditional values anchored in sustainable use of Our Diaspora resources.


We promote investment opportunities and savings among non-resident Kenyans particularly those residing and working in the United States of America, so that they can have access to reliable, dependable and organized ways to invest, access to affordable credit, carry out projects, build businesses, or meet personal and family needs in Kenya. The Sacco was founded out of needs and circumstances that non-resident Kenyans including those in the USA encounter every day.
